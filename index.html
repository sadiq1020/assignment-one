Our Donattion Plan


Our Sylhet Aid team will handout relief packages to affect homes which includes food packs and boats for essential travelling. We plan to help the most vulnerable people to access nutritious food and essential items as soon as possible.

Emergency Food Pack


Rice 10kg, Dall 2kg, Cooking Oil 1 Ltr, Sera 2kg, Muri 1kg, Powder Milk Half kg, Sugar 1 kg, Biscuits, Saline 1 Box, Candle 1box, Matches, Soap, Paracetamol & Antihistamine

Medical treatment


Money from this sector, we will use it to buy emergency medical supplies, first aid kits and also various types of medicine. We will try to show these activities via facebook.

Boats for essential travel


We will use these donated money, from this sector, to buy as many small boats as possible for transportation purposes in the flood affected area.
